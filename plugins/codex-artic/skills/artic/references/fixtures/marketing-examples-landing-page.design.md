# Marketing Examples Landing Page Pattern Notes

## Reusable Patterns
- Lead with a value-focused headline that says the user outcome before the mechanism.
- Use the subtitle to explain how the product creates the outcome in plain language.
- Place product-in-action visuals near the first CTA so visitors understand the offer before scrolling.
- Pair each major claim with adjacent proof, reassurance, or objection-handling copy.

## Components
- Hero with one primary call-to-value and a quieter secondary proof or demo link.
- Product screenshot or interactive preview that reinforces the headline promise.
- Pricing, FAQ, and social proof blocks that answer conversion objections close to the decision point.

## Accessibility
- Keep CTA labels specific, readable, and distinguishable from secondary actions.
- Preserve strong heading order and body copy contrast so clarity does not depend on decorative visuals.
